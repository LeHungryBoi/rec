433MHz Signal Capture Viewer

Not connected to device

Instructions:

  • Upload the Pico code to your device
  • Press "Connect to device" and select your Pico serial port
  • Press buttons on your 433MHz remote control
  • Each captured signal will be displayed as a waveform with different colors
  • Waveforms are vertically offset for easy comparison

Note: This requires Chrome or Edge version 89+ with Web Serial API support.
